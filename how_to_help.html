Here are a few ways to help save captive tigers.

Sign a petition

A petition can be a good way to show widespread support for a cause. By signing the petition below, we can show to Congress that we want to rid the use of wild animals in circuses.

Sign this petition

Don't support circuses that use wild animals

There are many circuses that provide entertainment without the abuse of wild animals. Show your support by attending these circuses while boycotting circuses like Barnum & Bailey.
